Three Notable Antagonist

These characters, along with others, contribute to the tension and conflicts within Durarara!!, making the storyline more intriguing and unpredictable.

Izaya Orihara

A manipulative and enigmatic information broker who enjoys stirring up chaos and conflict among the residents of Ikebukuro.

A skilled and deadly assassin who is hired to eliminate targets. She becomes involved in the conflicts of Ikebukuro and presents a formidable challenge to the main characters.

While not traditionally seen as an antagonist, Shizuo is known for his explosive and violent temper. He often clashes with other characters and becomes a source of trouble and conflict.
